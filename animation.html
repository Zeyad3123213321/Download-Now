Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our template

Do I need After Effects experience to use this template?

Basic knowledge of After Effects is helpful but not required. Our template comes with detailed video tutorials and documentation that walk you through the entire process, even if you're a beginner.

What version of After Effects do I need?

The template is compatible with After Effects CC 2018 and later versions. We recommend using the latest version of After Effects for the best performance and compatibility.

Are the fonts used in the template included?

All fonts used in the template are free and links to download them are included in the documentation. We've chosen high-quality, free fonts to keep the template accessible.

Can I use this template for client projects?

Absolutely! The template comes with a commercial license that allows you to use it for client projects. You can create unlimited projects for yourself or your clients.

How do I get support if I need help?

We provide dedicated support through our helpdesk system. Depending on your license, you'll receive 6 months to lifetime support. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.
